About Pokemano

The goal of this website is for Pokemon players to have a place to compare Pokemon.

Our page consists of a Pokedex, where you can search for Pokemon by ID or by Name, and look up all the available information on said Pokemon.

You can also compare stats like Attack, Defense and HP of two individual Pokemon and see which one is best for you!

Thank you for using Pokemano, we hope you enjoy it.
